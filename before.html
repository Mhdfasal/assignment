Before a Disaster

Create an Emergency Plan

Ensure all family members know what to do in case of an emergency. This includes evacuation routes and meeting points.

Prepare an Emergency Kit

Include essentials like water, non-perishable food, medications, first aid supplies, flashlights, batteries, and important documents.

Stay Informed

Keep up-to-date with weather forecasts and warnings through reliable sources.

Secure Your Home

Reinforce windows, doors, and roofs. Secure heavy furniture and appliances to prevent them from tipping over.

Know Your Area

Be aware of the types of disasters that are common in your area and prepare accordingly.
