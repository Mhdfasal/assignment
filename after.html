After a Disaster

Check for Injuries

Provide first aid and seek medical help if needed.

Inspect Your Home

Check for structural damage, gas leaks, and other hazards before re-entering.

Avoid Contaminated Water

Do not drink tap water until it has been declared safe.

Clean Up Safely

Wear protective gear and follow guidelines for cleaning up debris and hazardous materials.

Seek Support

Reach out to community resources and support networks for assistance and emotional support.
